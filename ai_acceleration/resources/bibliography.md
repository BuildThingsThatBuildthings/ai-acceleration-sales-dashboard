## Citation Standards Used

### Format: APA 7th Edition

**General Format**:
- Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work*. Publisher. URL

**In-Text Citations**:
- First use: Federal Bureau of Investigation [FBI], 2024
- Subsequent: FBI, 2024
- Numbered references: [FBI-1] for multiple sources from same organization

### Verification Requirements

All citations must meet these standards:
- ✅ **Authoritative**: Government, academic, industry association, or recognized research organization
- ✅ **Current**: Published within last 24 months (preferably); older sources for foundational concepts only
- ✅ **Accessible**: Publicly available URL or widely accessible database
- ✅ **Verifiable**: Claims can be independently verified from source
- ✅ **Relevant**: Directly supports claim made in course materials

### Citation Placement

**Research Documents**: Inline citations with numbered references (e.g., [FBI-1])

**Handouts**: Footnotes at bottom of page (e.g., [^1])

**Slide Decks**: Source attribution on slide or in speaker notes

**Instructor Guide**: Full bibliography reference in appendix
