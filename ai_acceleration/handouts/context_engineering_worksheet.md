# Context Engineering Worksheet
**Build Your Voice & Tactics Card**

---

## What Is This?

Your **Voice & Tactics Card** is a reusable document you paste at the top of every AI prompt. It teaches AI your specific voice, objections, local knowledge, and compliance rules — once.

**Build it once → Use it forever → Update quarterly**

**Time to complete:** 15-20 minutes
**Time saved per use:** 5-10 minutes (AI gets it right the first time)

### Standard Compliance Reminders:
- **Fair Housing:** No language suggesting preference or limitation based on race, color, national origin, religion, sex, disability, or familial status
- **Verifiable Facts:** All property details must be accurate and sourced
- **Avoid Steering:** Describe property, not ideal occupant
- **Material Defects:** Disclose known issues accurately

## How to Use Your Voice & Tactics Card

### **Option 1: Paste at Top of Every Prompt (Recommended)**
```
[PASTE YOUR COMPLETE VOICE & TACTICS CARD HERE]

Now, using the context above, [your specific prompt]
```

### **Option 2: Save as Snippet/Template**
- Create keyboard shortcut (e.g., ";;context") that expands to full card
- Save in ChatGPT custom instructions (Settings → Personalization)
- Keep in dedicated doc/note for copy/paste

### **Option 3: Reference by Name**
"Use my Voice & Tactics Card from [date]. Then write [task]."
(Only works if you've used it in same conversation thread)

## Completed Card Example

```
VOICE & TACTICS CARD - Sarah Johnson Real Estate

Voice Pillars:
- Data-driven and transparent
- Warm but professional
- Community-focused, long-term relationships

Taboo Phrases:
Never: "Stunning," "Dream home," "Motivated seller," demographic descriptors
Avoid: Exclamation points (use sparingly), hype language, unverifiable claims
Always: Lead with facts, specific over generic, verify everything

Top Objections & Responses:
1. "Rates are too high" → Show rent vs. buy math, equity building over time
2. "Should I wait for prices to drop?" → Historical data, cost of waiting analysis
3. "Why list with you?" → Track record (8% higher sale price avg), marketing strategy, negotiation expertise

Local Nuance:
- Williamson County schools: Top-rated, major buyer draw (9/10 avg)
- Franklin: 15 min to downtown, dining/culture hub
- Brentwood: Upscale, family-focused, strong HOAs
- Spring Hill: New construction, growing, commuter-friendly to Nashville (I-65)

CTAs:
Primary: "Schedule 15-min consultation" (calendar link)
Secondary: "Reply with your top 3 must-haves"

Compliance:
- No Fair Housing violations (avoid family-oriented, safe neighborhood, etc.)
- All facts must be verifiable
- Disclose material defects accurately
```
