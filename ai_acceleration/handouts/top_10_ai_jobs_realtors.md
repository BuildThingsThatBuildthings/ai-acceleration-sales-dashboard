## The 10 Highest-Value AI Jobs

### **1. Listing Descriptions**
**What It Is:** Writing property descriptions for MLS, Zillow, social media

**Time Currently:** 20-40 minutes per listing
**Time With AI:** 5-10 minutes per listing
**Time Saved:** 15-30 min per listing

**What AI Does:**
- Turns bullet points into compelling prose
- Matches your brand voice with context card
- Creates variations for different channels (MLS, social, email)

**What You Do:**
- Provide accurate property facts
- Verify all details
- Check Fair Housing compliance
- Add personal touches if desired

**Selection Criteria Score:**
- ✓ Minutes/Week: HIGH (if you do 2+ listings/week)
- ✓ Repeatability: 85% (similar structure every time)
- ✓ Risk: LOW (you verify facts before publishing)
- ✓ Measurable: EASY (time saved per listing)
- ✓ Data Available: YES (from MLS, photos, your knowledge)

**Tools:** ChatGPT, Claude, Writer.Homes

**Quick Win Potential:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Selection Criteria Deep Dive

### **Use These 5 Filters to Pick Your First 2 Pilots:**

### **Filter 1: Minutes Per Week**
**Question:** How much time does this task consume weekly?

**Scoring:**
- 2+ hours/week = HIGH priority
- 1-2 hours/week = MODERATE priority
- <1 hour/week = LOW priority (pilot later)

**Your Answer:**
Job #1: _____________ = _____ hours/week
Job #2: _____________ = _____ hours/week

---

### **Filter 2: Repeatability**
**Question:** Is this the same task repeatedly, or unique every time?

**Scoring:**
- 80%+ similar = EXCELLENT (listing descriptions, email responses)
- 60-80% similar = GOOD (social posts, CMAs)
- <60% similar = POOR pilot candidate (custom negotiations)

**Your Answer:**
Job #1: _____________ = _____% repeatability
Job #2: _____________ = _____% repeatability

---

### **Filter 3: Risk Level**
**Question:** What happens if AI output has an error?

**Scoring:**
- LOW: Social media, initial drafts you review = PILOT FIRST
- MEDIUM: Client communications, pricing = PILOT WITH CARE
- HIGH: Legal advice, contract terms = DON'T AUTOMATE

**Your Answer:**
Job #1: _____________ = LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH risk
Job #2: _____________ = LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH risk

---

### **Filter 4: Measurability**
**Question:** Can you easily measure time saved or quality improved?

**Scoring:**
- EASY: Time tracked (minutes per task) = BEST PILOTS
- MODERATE: Quality proxies (client feedback) = GOOD
- HARD: Soft benefits only = POOR pilot

**Your Answer:**
Job #1: _____________ = EASY / MODERATE / HARD to measure
Job #2: _____________ = EASY / MODERATE / HARD to measure

---

### **Filter 5: Data Availability**
**Question:** Do you have the inputs AI needs?

**Scoring:**
- YES: MLS data, your knowledge, client info = PILOT NOW
- PARTIAL: Need to gather some info first = PILOT SOON
- NO: Requires research or external data = NOT YET

**Your Answer:**
Job #1: _____________ = YES / PARTIAL / NO
Job #2: _____________ = YES / PARTIAL / NO
