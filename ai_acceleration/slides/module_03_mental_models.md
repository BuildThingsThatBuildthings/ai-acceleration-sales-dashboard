# Module 3: Mental Models for AI

---

## Mental Models: Your AI Cheat Codes

**Story**: Two agents both try AI for listing descriptions.

- One gives up in 10 minutes: "It doesn't work"
- The other generates 5 great listings in 20 minutes

**Same tool. What's the difference?**

Mental models.

> Mental models are cheat codes for your brain - they help you see patterns fast

---

## Mental Model 1: AI as "Smart Intern"

**The Model**: AI has read everything but needs clear instructions and supervision

**Key Insight**: You wouldn't send an intern to a client meeting alone - don't publish AI content without review

**Real Estate Example**:
- AI drafts your listing description fast
- YOU verify accuracy (sqft, beds/baths, school zones)

**Process**: AI → Draft → Human Review → Publish

> **Catch phrase**: "AI does the first draft, you do the final cut"

---

## Mental Model 2: Trust But Verify

**The Model**: AI will confidently state things that are wrong (hallucinations)

**Key Insight**: Verify every fact, especially numbers, addresses, claims

**Real Estate Example**:
- AI might say "close to top-rated schools" when there are no schools nearby
- ALWAYS fact-check before sending to clients

**Warning Signs**:
- Specific numbers (sqft, prices, dates)
- Local market claims
- School ratings or zones
- Neighborhood descriptions

> **Catch phrase**: "If AI says it, verify it"

---

## Mental Model 3: Context Over Cleverness

**The Model**: AI quality depends on context you provide, not prompt cleverness

**Key Insight**: More specifics = better output. Generic prompts = generic results

**Example Comparison**:

**Bad Prompt**: "Write a listing description"

**Good Prompt**: "Write a 150-word listing for 123 Main St, 3bd/2ba ranch, updated kitchen, cul-de-sac lot, Williamson schools, target first-time buyers, friendly but professional tone"

**Result**: Good prompt = 10x better output

> **Catch phrase**: "Feed it details, get back quality"

---

## Mental Model 4: OODA Loop (Small Iterations)

**The Model**: Observe → Orient → Decide → Act (repeat)

**Key Insight**: Don't expect perfection on first try - iterate fast

**Real Estate Workflow**:
1. **Generate** first draft (30 seconds)
2. **Review** what's wrong/missing (30 seconds)
3. **Refine** with follow-up prompt: "Make it shorter" or "Add curb appeal details" (30 seconds)
4. **Repeat** until excellent (2-3 cycles)

**Total time**: 3-5 minutes for polished content

**Not AI failure**: Iteration is the process

> **Catch phrase**: "Ship small, iterate fast"

---

## Mental Model 5: Scaling Excellence

**The Model**: AI amplifies your best people's knowledge across your entire team

**Key Insight**: AI doesn't replace expertise - it multiplies it

**Real Estate Application**:
- Top producer's objection handling → Template for whole team
- Senior agent's market knowledge → Consistent messaging for everyone
- Your proven email sequences → Instant access for new hires

**The Power**: Turn your best practices into AI-powered systems

**The Vision**: AI makes everyone perform at top-producer level

> **Catch phrase**: "Scale your best, automate the rest"

---

## 5 Mental Models Recap

1. **Smart Intern**: AI drafts, you direct and verify
2. **Trust But Verify**: Always fact-check before publishing
3. **Context Over Cleverness**: Detailed prompts = quality outputs
4. **OODA Loop**: Iterate fast, don't expect perfection v1
5. **Scaling Excellence**: Multiply top-performer knowledge across team

**Your Mission**: Apply these models to every AI interaction
