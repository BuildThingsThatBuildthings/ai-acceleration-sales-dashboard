# Marketing Department SOP

## Overview
This directive outlines the standard operating procedures for the Marketing Department, following the 3-layer architecture.

## Goals
- Promote "AI Acceleration" and other products.
- Execute marketing campaigns efficiently.
- Track and analyze performance.

## Workflow
1.  **Campaign Planning:**
    - Define goals, audience, and channels.
    - Create a campaign-specific directive in `directives/marketing/`.
2.  **Content Creation:**
    - Use `execution/marketing/generate_content.py` (to be created) for automated content generation.
    - Review and refine content.
3.  **Execution:**
    - Schedule and publish content.
    - Monitor engagement.
4.  **Review:**
    - Analyze metrics.
    - Update directives based on learnings.

## Tools
- `execution/marketing/` scripts.
