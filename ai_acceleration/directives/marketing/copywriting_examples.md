# Copywriting Examples & Context

## Tone & Style
- **Casual & Natural:** Write as if you are texting a colleague. Avoid "marketing speak."
- **Direct:** Get to the point immediately.
- **Researched:** Show, don't just tell, that you know who they are.

## "Casualization" Examples
| Raw Company Name | Casualized Version | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Keller Williams Realty, Inc. | Keller Williams | Drops legal suffix |
| Re/Max Fine Properties | Re/Max | Drops specific branch name |
| Coldwell Banker Global Luxury | Coldwell Banker | Drops sub-brand unless relevant |
| Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices | Berkshire Hathaway | Shortens for flow |
| The Ryan Wanner Team at eXp | eXp (or "your team") | Focuses on the main entity |
