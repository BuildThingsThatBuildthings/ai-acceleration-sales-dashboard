#### **2:10-2:40** | MODULE 9: AI Security & Fraud Awareness (30 min)
**Purpose**: Practical fraud detection and prevention

**Content**:
- **Reality Check**: $503M stolen in RE wire fraud (2023)

  2. **Voice Fraud**: Cloning (3-second audio requirement)
  3. **Image Fraud**: Fake listings, manipulated photos

- **Universal Rule**: Verify via known channel (NEVER act on email alone)

**Exercise**: ğŸ‹ï¸ **Fraud Demos** (15 min)
