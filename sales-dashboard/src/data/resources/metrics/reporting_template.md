## Who Gets What Report

| Report | Frequency | Recipient | Purpose |
|--------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Quick Stats | Daily | Self | Track progress |
| Weekly Summary | Friday | Stakeholders | Status update |
| Monthly Summary | End of month | Leadership | Performance review |
| Pipeline Report | Weekly | Sales manager | Deal tracking |
