# Weekly Review Framework

## Purpose

Every Friday, take 30 minutes to:
1. Analyze your performance
2. Identify what's working
3. Fix what's not
4. Plan next week

## Analysis Questions

### Performance Analysis

**If you exceeded targets:**
- What did you do differently?
- Which days were strongest?
- What lead type converted best?
- Can you replicate next week?

**If you missed targets:**
- Where did you fall short? (Activity or results?)
- What was the root cause?
- Was it within your control?
- What will you change?

### Funnel Analysis

**If connect rate dropped:**
- Did call timing change?
- Was data quality worse?
- Are you reaching right people?

**If book rate dropped:**
- Are conversations longer/shorter?
- Which objection is blocking?
- Are you asking for the meeting?

### Regional Analysis

**Questions to answer:**
- Which region is most receptive?
- Should you shift focus?
- Is one region saturated?
- Any regional trends in objections?

## Week-Over-Week Comparison

### 4-Week Trend
```
               Wk 1    Wk 2    Wk 3    Wk 4    Trend
Calls           200     210     190     220     ↑
Conv            75      80      70      82      ↑
Booked          8       10      7       11      ↑
Connect %       37%     38%     37%     37%     →
Book %          10%     12%     10%     13%     ↑
```

### What the Trends Tell You

**Calls trending up:**
- You're putting in the work
- Check if quality is maintained

**Connect rate stable:**
- Good - consistency matters
- If low, investigate data/timing

**Book rate trending up:**
- Your skills are improving
- Keep doing what you're doing
