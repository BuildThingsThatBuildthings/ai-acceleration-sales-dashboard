# Vertical Playbook: Brokerages

## Target Profile

### Decision Makers
| Title | Priority | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| CEO / President | P1 | Final budget authority |
| Broker/Owner | P1 | Often same as CEO |
| Managing Broker | P1 | Day-to-day operations |
| VP of Operations | P2 | Focuses on efficiency |
| Training Director | P2 | Champions development |

### Company Characteristics
- 5-100+ agents (sweet spot: 10-50)
- Independent or franchise
- Active in residential, commercial, or both
- Have budget for professional development

---

## Pain Points (What Keeps Them Up at Night)

### 1. Agent Productivity Variance
```
Problem: Top 20% do 80% of deals
Why it matters: Hard to scale, inconsistent revenue
Our solution: AI systematizes what top agents do intuitively
```

### 2. Recruiting Competitive Advantage
```
Problem: Agents can go anywhere
Why it matters: Retention is expensive, turnover hurts
Our solution: "We have AI systems your last brokerage didn't"
```

### 3. Training That Doesn't Stick
```
Problem: Webinars forgotten in a week
Why it matters: Wasted investment, frustrated leadership
Our solution: Hands-on workshop, agents build live, use Monday
```

### 4. Commission Splits Pressure
```
Problem: Agents want higher splits
Why it matters: Margins getting squeezed
Our solution: Offer value (AI training) instead of % points
```

### 5. Liability and Compliance
```
Problem: Agents saying wrong things, fraud risk
Why it matters: Lawsuits, reputation damage
Our solution: Fair Housing guardrails, fraud detection training
```

## Messaging for Brokerages

### Primary Value Proposition
```
"Your top 20% of agents are doing 80% of the deals.
AI doesn't make average agents great—but it makes
your best agents even more productive.

And here's the recruiting pitch:
'We have AI systems your last brokerage didn't.'"
```

### Secondary Angles

**For productivity-focused:**
```
"Your agents are working 60 hours but losing 10 to admin.
Listings, emails, social, reports—AI handles that.
5-10 hours back per week, per agent."
```

**For recruiting-focused:**
```
"When top agents are interviewing, what do you offer?
Higher split? Everyone does that.
AI training they can't get elsewhere? That's differentiation."
```

**For compliance-focused:**
```
"Wire fraud hit $500M last year. Deepfakes are coming.
Are your agents trained to spot it?
We spend 30 minutes on fraud detection—it's not optional."
```

## Objections Specific to Brokerages

### "My agents are independent contractors—can't force them"
```
"You're right—you can't force them.
But you can make it mandatory for the office meeting
and call it 'professional development.'

The agents who want to grow will show up.
Those are the ones worth investing in."
```

### "We're a franchise—corporate handles training"
```
"Does corporate cover AI implementation?
Most franchise training is concepts, not hands-on.
We complement what corporate does—we're implementation.
Would it help if I talked to your regional support?"
```

### "High agent turnover—why invest?"
```
"Train the ones who stay.
Here's the flip: agents who learn AI become stickier.
They won't leave for a brokerage without these tools.
AI training is retention, not just development."
```

### "My agents aren't tech people"
```
"Neither are most. That's why we start simple.
Mental models first, not technical jargon.
By end of day, they're using AI for listings and emails.
We've trained agents who'd never heard of ChatGPT."
```

No corporate approval needed, no red tape.

```

```

AI helps your agents respond faster, create better content,

```

### Commercial Brokerage

"92% of commercial teams are piloting AI.
Market analysis, lease abstracts, prospect research—
AI transforms how your brokers work.
Are you ahead of that curve or catching up?"
```
