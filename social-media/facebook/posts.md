### Post 2: The Prompt That Changed Everything

One prompt. That's all it took.

I watched an agent go from spending 40 minutes on listing descriptions to 10 minutes.

The prompt wasn't complicated. It was just... structured.

Here's the framework:

"You are a luxury real estate copywriter. Write a listing description for [address].

Details:
- [Beds/baths/sqft]
- [Key features]
- [Neighborhood highlights]

Voice: Confident but not salesy. Emphasize lifestyle, not just features. 150-200 words."

That's it. Context. Details. Voice guidelines.

Most agents type "write listing description" and wonder why the output is garbage.

The tool isn't broken. The instructions are.

AI can't build the trust that comes from showing up, again and again.

The best technology in the world doesn't change human nature.

People buy from people they trust.
AI just gives you more time to build that trust.
