## Usage Guide

### How to Film These

1. **Lighting:** Natural light or ring light. Face the light.
2. **Background:** Clean. Uncluttered. Or green screen.
3. **Eye contact:** Look at the camera lens, not the screen.
4. **Energy:** Higher than feels comfortable.
5. **Pace:** Fast. Cut the dead air.
6. **Captions:** Add them in TikTok or CapCut.

### Posting Strategy

- **Test:** Post 3-5 per day for first two weeks
- **Analyze:** See what hooks work best
- **Double down:** Make variations of winners
- **Repurpose:** Post best performers to Reels and Shorts
