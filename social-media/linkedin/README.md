# LinkedIn Platform Guide

Research-based best practices for B2B real estate content on LinkedIn (Updated December 2025).

---

## Why LinkedIn for This Business

- **277% more B2B leads** than Facebook + Twitter combined
- **75% of buyers** influenced by thought leadership content
- **52% of decision-makers** spend 1+ hours/week reading thought leadership
- Primary platform for reaching broker-owners and association directors

---

## Visual Guidelines

**Color Usage:**
- Primary backgrounds: White or Dark Red (#2D0A0A)
- Headlines: Crimson Red (#C41E3A)
- Accent numbers/stats: Champagne Gold (#D4AF37)
- Supporting elements: Sky Blue (#87CEEB)
- Body text: Charcoal (#374151)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Inter Semi-Bold, tight tracking
- Body: Inter Regular, generous line-height
- Stats: Inter Bold, large size

**Design Principle:**
Simple and elegant. Future centric, old school at heart. White space is luxury. Every element earns its place.

## Algorithm Insights (November 2025 Update)

### What Wins Now

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Comment depth | Prioritized over likes - meaningful conversations win |
| Multi-image posts | 6.60% avg engagement (highest of all formats) |
| Polls | Dominate impressions - every vote spreads the post |
| Native content | Off-platform links are penalized |
| Keywords | Now more important than hashtags for discovery |
| Post lifespan | 2-3 weeks if engagement stays strong |

### What to Avoid

- External links in main post (put in comments instead)
- Engagement bait ("Like if you agree!")
- Pure promotional content without value
- Posting without engaging on comments

## Content Strategy

### Format Rankings (by engagement)

1. **Multi-image posts** - 6.60% engagement
2. **Polls** - Highest impressions
3. **Carousels** - Educational content powerhouse
4. **Native video** - 5x more engagement than text
5. **Text posts** - Still strong if hooks are good

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | % of Posts | Purpose |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Market Intelligence | 25% | AI adoption trends, industry stats |
| Pain Point Content | 30% | The AI paradox, time waste |
| Social Proof | 20% | Testimonials, case studies |
| Educational | 15% | Quick tips, tool reviews |
| Promotional | 10% | Workshop announcements |

## Posting Schedule

**Frequency:** 4-5 posts per week (Mon-Fri)

**Best Times:**
- Tuesday-Thursday: 9 AM - 2 PM
- Peak windows: 10-11 AM and 12 PM (lunch)

**First 30-60 Minutes:** Critical for algorithm. Engage immediately with any comments.

---

## Post Structure

### The Hook (First 2-3 Lines)

Must stop the scroll. Options:
- Bold statement or stat
- Controversial opinion
- Intriguing question
- Story opener

**Examples:**
- "68 brokers walked into a room. All 68 said they 'use AI.'"
- "I asked 50 broker-owners one question. Only 17% had a good answer."
- "Unpopular opinion: Most AI training is useless."

### The Body

- Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- Use â†’ bullets for lists
- Include specific numbers
- Tell stories, not lectures

### The CTA

- Soft: "What's been your experience?"
- Medium: "Comment [WORD] for the guide"
- Hard: "DM me 'WORKSHOP' for dates"

## Hashtag Strategy

**2025 Update:** Keywords now matter more than hashtags for discovery.

**Use 3-5 hashtags max:**
- #realestatebrokerage
- #brokerlife
- #realestateai
- #aitraining
- #brokerowner

Put hashtags at the end, not inline.

---

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Tasks (15-20 min)
1. Respond to all comments on your posts
2. Comment meaningfully on 5-10 posts from target audience
3. Reply to DMs within 24 hours

### Comment Strategy
- Ask follow-up questions
- Add additional value
- Never just say "Great post!"
- Aim for 10+ word comments

---

## Employee Advocacy

**40% higher engagement** when content comes from personal profiles vs. company pages.

Strategy:
- Train team members to share and comment
- Create shareable content they can personalize
- Celebrate team engagement publicly

---

## What Makes Posts Viral

Based on analysis of top-performing B2B posts:

1. **Specific numbers** beat vague claims
2. **Personal stories** outperform corporate messaging
3. **Contrarian takes** spark debate (comments boost reach)
4. **Before/after transformations** drive saves and shares
5. **Behind-the-scenes** content humanizes the brand

---

## Common Mistakes

1. **Leading with the sell** - Give value first, always
2. **Walls of text** - Use whitespace and short paragraphs
3. **Generic advice** - Be specific to real estate
4. **No hook** - First line must stop the scroll
5. **Ignoring comments** - Reply to everything
6. **Posting and ghosting** - Engage for 30 min after posting
