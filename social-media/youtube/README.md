# YouTube Platform Guide

Research-based best practices for YouTube content targeting real estate professionals (Updated December 2025).

---

## Why YouTube for This Business

- **2nd largest search engine** after Google
- **Long-form content** builds deep trust and authority
- **Evergreen discovery** - videos rank for years
- **YouTube Shorts** for discovery, long-form for conversion
- **Professional audience** researches on YouTube before buying

---

## Visual Guidelines

**Color Usage:**
- Primary backgrounds: White or Dark Red (#2D0A0A)
- Headlines/Titles: Crimson Red (#C41E3A)
- Accent numbers/stats: Champagne Gold (#D4AF37)
- Supporting elements: Sky Blue (#87CEEB)
- Thumbnail text: High contrast (white on dark, red on light)

**Typography:**
- Thumbnails: Bold, 3-5 words max, highly readable
- Lower thirds: Clean, consistent placement
- On-screen text: Large enough for mobile viewing

**Design Principle:**
Simple and elegant. Future centric, old school at heart. Thumbnails must pop while maintaining brand sophistication.

## Algorithm Insights (2025)

### Key Ranking Signals

| Signal | Weight | How to Win |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Watch Time | Critical | Keep viewers watching longer |
| Click-Through Rate (CTR) | Very High | Thumbnails + titles matter |
| Audience Retention | High | >50% retention = algorithm love |
| Session Time | High | Videos that lead to more watching |
| Engagement (likes, comments) | Medium | Ask for interaction |

### The New Viewer Journey

1. **Shorts:** Discovery tool for new audiences
2. **Suggested Videos:** Algorithm serves your content to interested viewers
3. **Search:** Evergreen traffic from keyword-optimized content
4. **Subscriptions:** Your base for launches and announcements

## Content Strategy

### Two-Track Approach

**Track 1: YouTube Shorts (Discovery)**
- Under 60 seconds
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Hook-first content
- Goal: Get new eyes on your channel

**Track 2: Long-Form Tutorials (Authority)**
- 10-20 minutes
- Horizontal format (16:9)
- Deep value content
- Goal: Build trust and convert to leads

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | % of Content | Format |
|--------|--------------|--------|
| Educational | 50% | Tutorials, how-tos |
| Thought Leadership | 25% | Industry takes, predictions |
| Social Proof | 15% | Case studies, results |
| Promotional | 10% | Workshop info, CTAs |

## Long-Form Video Best Practices

### Optimal Length

| Length | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| 8-12 min | Standard tutorials |
| 15-20 min | Comprehensive guides |
| 20-30 min | Deep dives, courses |

### Video Structure

**The Hook (0:00-0:30)**
- State the problem/promise immediately
- Why should they keep watching?
- No long intros or logos

**The Setup (0:30-2:00)**
- What they'll learn
- Why you're credible
- Quick overview

**The Content (2:00-end)**
- Deliver on the promise
- Use timestamps for navigation
- Break into clear sections

**The CTA (final 30-60 sec)**
- What should they do next?
- Subscribe, comment, link in description

### Production Quality

| Element | Minimum Standard |
|---------|------------------|
| Audio | Clear, no echo (mic required) |
| Lighting | Well-lit face, no harsh shadows |
| Camera | 1080p minimum, 4K preferred |
| Background | Clean, professional, uncluttered |

## YouTube Shorts Strategy

### What Works

- **First frame = hook** (viewers swipe fast)
- **Vertical only** (9:16 aspect ratio)
- **Under 60 seconds** (ideally 15-45 sec)
- **Text on screen** (most watch muted)
- **Loop potential** (creates replays)

### Shorts Content Ideas

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Quick tip | "One prompt that saves 10 hours" |
| Myth bust | "AI isn't replacing agents" |
| Hot take | "Most AI training is useless" |
| Story clip | Excerpt from longer video |
| Before/after | "Writing listings before vs. after AI" |

### Shorts to Long-Form Pipeline

1. Post Shorts that get views
2. Create longer versions of winners
3. Reference long-form in Shorts
4. Use Shorts as trailers for tutorials

## Thumbnail Best Practices

### What Gets Clicks

- **Faces with emotion** (surprise, curiosity, frustration)
- **Large, readable text** (3-5 words max)
- **High contrast colors** (yellow, red on dark backgrounds)
- **Before/after format** (split screen)
- **Clean, uncluttered** (one focal point)

### What to Avoid

- Generic stock photos
- Too much text
- Low contrast
- Busy backgrounds
- Clickbait that doesn't deliver

### Thumbnail Checklist

- [ ] Readable at mobile size
- [ ] Face visible (if using)
- [ ] Text under 5 words
- [ ] High contrast
- [ ] Matches title promise

## Title Optimization

### Title Formulas That Work

1. **How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]**
   - "How to Write Listings in 5 Minutes with AI"

2. **[Number] [things] to [achieve result]**
   - "5 AI Prompts Every Real Estate Agent Needs"

3. **Why [common belief] is Wrong**
   - "Why Most AI Training Fails (And What Works)"

4. **The [type] Guide to [topic]**
   - "The Complete Guide to AI for Real Estate Brokers"

5. **I [did something] - Here's What Happened**
   - "I Trained 50 Agents on AI - Here's What Worked"

### Title Checklist

- [ ] Under 60 characters
- [ ] Main keyword near beginning
- [ ] Clear benefit or curiosity
- [ ] Matches thumbnail

## Description Optimization

### Structure

```
[First 2 lines: Hook + what they'll learn]

[Timestamps:]
0:00 - Intro
1:30 - Topic 1
5:00 - Topic 2
...

[About this video:]
2-3 sentences expanding on content

[Links:]
- Free resource
- Workshop info
- Social links

[Keywords at end - natural paragraph]
```

### SEO Tips

- Include main keyword in first 2 lines
- Use timestamps (YouTube favors them)
- Add relevant links
- Include keyword-rich closing paragraph

## Common Mistakes

1. **Long intros** - Hook in first 5 seconds
2. **No clear structure** - Use timestamps
3. **Bad audio** - Invest in a mic
4. **Boring thumbnails** - Test variations
5. **No CTA** - Tell viewers what to do
6. **Inconsistent posting** - Build a schedule
7. **Ignoring Shorts** - Missing discovery channel
8. **Not optimizing titles** - Keywords matter
