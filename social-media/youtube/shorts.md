## Filming Guide

### Equipment Needed

- Phone (vertical, front camera)
- Ring light or window
- Quiet space
- Teleprompter app (optional)

### Quick Tips

1. **Energy up** - More than feels natural
2. **Eye contact** - Look at lens, not screen
3. **Quick cuts** - Trim dead air
4. **Captions** - Add in CapCut or YouTube
5. **Batch film** - Do 5-10 in one session
