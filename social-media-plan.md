# AI Acceleration Workshop: 12-Week Social Media Plan

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive social media strategy targets real estate brokerages, associations, and decision-makers for the $5,000 6-Hour AI Masterclass and $3,000 3-Hour Implementation Workshop. Based on 2025 research showing that 87% of realtors use Facebook, 62% use Instagram, 48% use LinkedIn, and 25% use YouTube, this plan prioritizes multi-platform presence with emphasis on thought leadership content.

**Key Statistics Driving This Strategy:**
- LinkedIn generates 277% more leads than Facebook and Twitter combined for B2B
- 68% of real estate agents have adopted AI, but only 17% report significant positive impact (massive opportunity gap)
- Video content receives 403% more inquiries on real estate listings
- 75% of buyers are influenced by thought leadership content
- 52% of decision-makers spend 1+ hours/week reading thought leadership

## Part 1: Platform-Specific Strategies

### LinkedIn (Primary B2B Platform)

**Target Audience:** Broker-Owners, Managing Brokers, Association Executive Directors, Education Directors

**Posting Schedule:**
- **Frequency:** 4-5x per week (Mon-Fri)
- **Optimal Times:** 7-9 AM and 9 AM-3 PM Tuesday-Thursday
- **Content Types:** Long-form posts, carousels, video (5x more engagement), articles, newsletters

**Content Pillars:**
1. **Market Intelligence** (25%) - AI adoption trends, industry statistics
2. **Pain Point Content** (30%) - Address the "AI Paradox" (68% adopted, only 17% see results)
3. **Social Proof** (20%) - Testimonials, case studies, workshop results
4. **Educational Content** (15%) - Quick AI tips, tool reviews
5. **Promotional** (10%) - Workshop announcements, early-bird offers

**LinkedIn-Specific Tactics:**
- Use Sales Navigator for targeting broker-owners
- Comment on industry posts for 15 min/day
- Publish monthly newsletters
- Run Lead Gen Ads targeting "Broker," "Managing Director," "Association Executive"

---

### Instagram (Visual Storytelling + Reels)

**Target Audience:** Younger broker-owners, tech-forward agents, aspiring brokers

**Posting Schedule:**
- **Frequency:** 1-2x per day (feed + stories)
- **Optimal Times:** 11 AM-1 PM and 7-9 PM
- **Content Types:** Reels (primary), Carousels (4.1% engagement rate), Stories

**Content Pillars:**
1. **Behind-the-Scenes** (25%) - Workshop prep, day-in-the-life
2. **Quick Tips** (30%) - 30-second AI tutorials
3. **Transformation Stories** (20%) - Before/after results
4. **Trending Audio Reels** (15%) - Hook to trending sounds
5. **Promotional** (10%) - CTA to link in bio

**Instagram-Specific Tactics:**
- Use location tags within 5-15 mile radius for local distribution
- Collaborate with local real estate influencers
- Create highlight reels: "AI Tips," "Testimonials," "Workshop," "Results"
- Use carousel posts for step-by-step guides
