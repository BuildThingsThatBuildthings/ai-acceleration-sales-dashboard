## Brand Overview

### The Business

**Name:** AI Acceleration Workshop (working title)

**Tagline Options:**
- "Implementation, Not Webinars"
- "Turn AI Into an Operational Asset"
- "Where AI Meets Real Estate Reality"
- "The 17% Advantage"

**What We Do:** Hands-on AI training for real estate brokerages that transforms how teams operate. Not theory. Not lectures. Agents leave with working systems.

### Products

| Product | Price | Duration | Capacity | Positioning |
|---------|-------|----------|----------|-------------|
| AI Masterclass | $5,000 | 6 hours | 20 seats | Primary offer |
| Implementation Workshop | $3,000 | 3 hours | 20 seats | Downsell |

### Target Market

**Geography:** Nashville (TN) to Hershey (PA) corridor
- Tennessee, Pennsylvania, Virginia, Kentucky, Maryland, West Virginia

**Decision Makers:**
- Broker-Owners
- Managing Brokers
- Association Executive Directors
- Education Directors

**The Container:** We target brokerages, associations, and trade groups. NOT individual agents.

### Brand Positioning

**The Core Problem We Solve:**
68% of real estate agents have adopted AI.
Only 17% report significant positive impact.
**51% are stuck in the gap.** We close that gap.

**Our Unique Angle:**
AI makes agents MORE human, not less. AI handles speed (admin, content, follow-ups). Agents handle connection (relationships, trust, showing up).

### Design Philosophy: Future Centric, Old School at Heart

**The Balance:**
Our aesthetic marries forward-thinking technology with timeless principles. We're not chasing trends—we're building on what has always worked while embracing tools that make it faster.

| Future Centric | Old School at Heart |
|----------------|---------------------|
| AI tools, efficiency metrics | Relationships first |
| Competitive advantage, speed | Trust earned over time |
| Data-driven decisions | Human judgment matters |
| Modern workflows | Proven principles |

**Visual Expression:**
- Clean typography with classic proportions (timeless, not trendy)
- Crimson Red = bold action, old school confidence
- Champagne Gold = timeless luxury, not flashy tech gold
- Sky Blue = trustworthy technology, approachable not cold
- Generous white space = elegance over information density
- Simple and uncluttered—every element earns its place

**Voice Expression:**
- Lead with data, land on relationships
- Technology serves humanity, not replaces it
- Experienced wisdom + modern tools = competitive edge
- "The best brokers always knew this. Now they have better tools."

**The Old School Principle:**
> AI doesn't replace good judgment. It protects the time needed to exercise it.

**Length:** 60 seconds
**Aspect Ratios:** 16:9 (YouTube/website), 9:16 (TikTok/Reels), 1:1 (LinkedIn)

**Script:**

```
[0:00-0:05] HOOK
Visual: Split screen - chaos on left (paper stacks, stressed broker), calm on right (clean desk, AI dashboard)

[0:05-0:10] THE PROBLEM
Visual: Number "17%" animating dramatically

Visual: Transition to bright, clean workshop environment

[0:25-0:40] THE SOLUTION

Visual: Screen recordings showing AI tools in action - text generating, reports building

Visual: Logo, workshop date/location, booking URL
```

### Video Style Guide

**Visual Language:**
- Clean, uncluttered frames
- Natural lighting, warm but professional
- B-roll and setup shots only (no testimonials until we have real ones)
- Screen recordings when demonstrating AI tools
- Minimal text on screen (let VO carry message)

**Transitions:**
- Clean cuts preferred
- Subtle zoom for emphasis
- Avoid: Wipes, spins, cheesy transitions

**Graphics:**
- Lower thirds: Crimson red (#C41E3A) background, gold accent line, white text
- Statistics: Large numbers in gold (#D4AF37), animated growth/reveal
- Logo: End card, 3-5 seconds minimum

**Audio:**
- Music bed: Subtle, doesn't compete with VO
- VO: Confident, conversational, NOT salesy
- Natural sound: Workshop ambiance for authenticity

**IMPORTANT: No Testimonials**
Do not include testimonial clips, quotes, or implied results from past clients. We have not yet conducted workshops. All claims must be based on industry statistics (NAR data on the 68%/17% gap) or general capability statements, not customer success stories.

### Typography

#### Primary Typeface: Inter (or Satoshi)

**Why:** Clean, modern, highly readable, excellent at all sizes, professional but not cold.

**Fallback Stack:** `'Inter', 'Satoshi', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, sans-serif`

#### Type Scale

| Name | Size | Weight | Line Height | Use |
|------|------|--------|-------------|-----|
| **Display XL** | 72px / 4.5rem | 700 | 1.1 | Hero headlines |
| **Display** | 48px / 3rem | 700 | 1.15 | Page titles |
| **H1** | 36px / 2.25rem | 600 | 1.2 | Section headers |
| **H2** | 30px / 1.875rem | 600 | 1.25 | Subsection headers |
| **H3** | 24px / 1.5rem | 600 | 1.3 | Card titles |
| **H4** | 20px / 1.25rem | 600 | 1.35 | Small headers |
| **Body Large** | 18px / 1.125rem | 400 | 1.6 | Lead paragraphs |
| **Body** | 16px / 1rem | 400 | 1.65 | Standard copy |
| **Body Small** | 14px / 0.875rem | 400 | 1.6 | Captions, labels |
| **Caption** | 12px / 0.75rem | 500 | 1.5 | Fine print |

#### Type Styles

**Headlines:**
- Weight: 600-700 (SemiBold to Bold)
- Color: Crimson Red (#C41E3A) or Charcoal (#374151)
- Letter-spacing: -0.02em (slight tightening)

**Body Copy:**
- Weight: 400 (Regular)
- Color: Charcoal (#374151)
- Line-height: 1.6-1.7 for readability
- Max width: 65-75 characters per line

**Accent Text (Stats, Numbers):**
- Weight: 700 (Bold)
- Color: Champagne Gold (#D4AF37) or Crimson Red (#C41E3A)
- May be larger than surrounding text

### Voice & Tone

#### Brand Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Direct** | No fluff, get to the point | "Your agents waste 14 hours a week." NOT "In today's rapidly evolving landscape..." |
| **Data-Driven** | Lead with numbers | "68% adopted, 17% see results" NOT "Many agents struggle with AI" |
| **Consultant** | Expert guiding, not selling | "Here's what actually works" NOT "Limited time offer!" |
| **Confident** | No hedging or uncertainty | "This works." NOT "This might help" |
| **Human** | Warm but professional | Use "you/your" often, acknowledge struggles |

#### Reading Level: 5th Grade

- Short sentences (15-20 words max)
- Simple words (avoid jargon)
- Active voice
- Bullet points over paragraphs

#### Words We Use

**Future Words:**
- Implementation (not deployment)
- Hands-on (not interactive)
- Working systems (not solutions)
- Transform (not change)
- Master (not learn)
- Operational asset (not tool)

**Old School Words (use to balance):**
- Relationships (alongside efficiency)
- Trust (not just speed)
- Experience (not just innovation)
- Fundamentals (not just features)
- Judgment (not just data)
- Connection (not just automation)

#### Words We Avoid

- Revolutionary, game-changing, disruptive
- Unlock, leverage, synergy
- Limited time, act now, don't miss out
- Best, #1, leading (unless provable)
- AI-powered (too generic)

### Photography Style

#### Subjects

**DO:**
- Workshop setup shots (empty room, clean space, equipment ready)
- Stock photos of real estate offices (properly licensed)
- Laptops/screens showing AI interfaces
- Diverse representation

**DON'T:**
- Photos implying past workshops we haven't done
- Stock photo handshakes
- Overly staged "tech" imagery
- Generic cityscape/skyline shots
- Robot/cyborg imagery
- Cheesy "success" poses (arms crossed, power stance)

#### Treatment

- **Lighting:** Natural preferred, warm tones
- **Color:** Slight desaturation, warm shadows
- **Composition:** Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- **Post-processing:** Minimal, authentic look

#### Image Overlays

When adding text to photos:
- Use crimson red (#C41E3A) or white text only
- Add semi-transparent overlay if needed (dark red #2D0A0A at 60-80%)
- Keep text in safe zones (rule of thirds)
- Ensure contrast meets WCAG AA (4.5:1 for body, 3:1 for large text)

### Iconography

**Style:** Line icons, consistent stroke weight

**Stroke:** 1.5px-2px depending on size

**Corner Radius:** Slightly rounded (2px at 24px size)

**Colors:** Crimson Red (#C41E3A) or White on dark backgrounds. Gold (#D4AF37) for special accents.

**Sets to Use:**
- Lucide Icons
- Heroicons (Outline style)

**Custom Icons Needed:**

## Usage Rules

### Logo Clear Space

Minimum clear space around logo = height of the icon portion

```
    ┌─────────────┐
  X │             │ X
    │   [LOGO]    │
  X │             │ X
    └─────────────┘

X = height of icon/mark
```

### Minimum Sizes

- Full logo: 120px wide minimum
- Icon only: 24px minimum

### Logo Misuse (Don'ts)

- Don't stretch or distort
- Don't change colors outside brand palette
- Don't add effects (shadows, glows, bevels)
- Don't rotate
- Don't put on busy/low-contrast backgrounds
- Don't outline
- Don't rearrange elements

### Color Application Rules

**Primary Color Ratios:**
- Red: 50% (hero)
- Gold: 20% (accent)
- Sky Blue: 20% (support)
- White: 10% (space)

**Gold Usage:**
- Premium accents (buttons, highlights, icons)
- Special CTAs and luxury elements
- Never for body text (contrast issues)

**Red Usage:**
- Primary CTAs and headers
- Brand statements and emphasis
- Video graphics and overlays

**Sky Blue Usage:**
- Tech/trust elements
- Informational content
- Secondary buttons and links

### Accessibility Requirements

**Contrast Ratios (WCAG AA):**
- Body text: 4.5:1 minimum
- Large text (18px+): 3:1 minimum
- Interactive elements: 3:1 minimum

**Approved Pairings:**
- Crimson Red (#C41E3A) on White: 5.9:1 ✓ (pass)
- White on Crimson Red: 5.9:1 ✓ (pass)
- Charcoal (#374151) on White: 7.5:1 ✓ (pass)
- White on Dark Red (#2D0A0A): 15.2:1 ✓ (pass)
- Champagne Gold (#D4AF37) on Dark Red: 6.8:1 ✓ (pass)
- Champagne Gold on White: 2.1:1 ✗ (FAIL - don't use for body text)
- Sky Blue (#87CEEB) on Dark Red: 8.4:1 ✓ (pass)

### Partner/Co-Branding

When appearing with partner logos:
- Equal visual weight
- Minimum 2X clear space between logos
- Use "in partnership with" text if needed
- Our logo always appears first or left
